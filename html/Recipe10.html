Banana Oat Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 120 g rolled oats
  • Optional: handful of dark chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mash the bananas in a bowl.
  3. Add the oats and mix until combined. Stir in chocolate chips if desired.
  4. Form small rounds on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  5. Bake for 15-18 minutes until cookies are golden.
  6. Let cool before eating.
