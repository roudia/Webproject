Carrot Tops Soup

Ingredients

  • Tops from one bunch of carrots
  • 2 potatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 1 vegetable bouillon cube
  • 750 ml water

Instructions

  1. Separate the carrot tops, wash thoroughly, and roughly chop them.
  2. Peel the potatoes and onion, then cut them into pieces.
  3. Sauté the onion in a large pot with a drizzle of olive oil for 3-4 minutes until translucent.
  4. Add the potatoes, carrot tops, water, and bouillon cube.
  5. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for about 20 minutes until vegetables are tender.
  6. Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until smooth. Season to taste and serve hot.
