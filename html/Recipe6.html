Vanilla Chia Pudding

Ingredients

  • 250 ml plant-based milk (almond, soy, oat...)
  • 3 tablespoons chia seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Fresh fruit for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Pour the plant milk into a bowl or jar.
  2. Add the chia seeds and vanilla extract. Whisk thoroughly.
  3. Let sit for 10 minutes, then stir again to prevent clumping.
  4. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight until the pudding thickens.
  5. Before serving, top with fresh fruit or nuts.
