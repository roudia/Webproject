Sugar-Free Energy Balls

Ingredients

  • 100 g pitted dates
  • 50 g almonds
  • 1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds

Instructions

  1. Place the dates and almonds in a food processor and blend until you obtain a thick, homogeneous paste.
  2. Add the cocoa powder and chia seeds and blend again until everything is well combined.
  3. With slightly damp hands, take small portions of the mixture and roll into walnut-sized balls.
  4. Place them on a plate and refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving.
