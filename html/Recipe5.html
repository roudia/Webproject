Zucchini Peel Spread

Ingredients

  • Peels from 2 organic zucchinis
  • 100 g cream cheese or spreadable cheese
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Rinse the zucchini peels and cut into pieces. Peel and finely chop the garlic.
  2. Sauté the peels with garlic in a small oiled pan for 5 minutes until soft.
  3. Let cool slightly, then blend with the cheese and lemon juice.
  4. Season with salt and pepper and blend again until creamy.
  5. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before serving, spread on bread or as a vegetable dip.
