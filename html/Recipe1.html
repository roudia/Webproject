Banana Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil

Instructions

  1. Peel the bananas and mash them in a large bowl using a fork until smooth.
  2. Crack the eggs into the bowl, add the cinnamon, and whisk everything until well combined.
  3. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and melt the coconut oil.
  4. Pour small amounts of batter to form pancakes about 8 to 10 cm in diameter.
  5. Cook each pancake for 2 to 3 minutes on each side, until golden brown.
  6. Serve immediately, optionally accompanied by fresh fruit or plain yogurt.
