Rice and Leftover Vegetable Patties

Ingredients

  • 00 g cooked rice
  • 100 g chopped leftover vegetables
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon flour
  • Spices or herbs (turmeric, paprika, parsley...)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix rice, vegetables, egg, flour, and spices.
  2. Form small patties with your hands. If the mixture is too wet, add a bit more flour.
  3. Heat a drizzle of oil in a pan.
  4. Cook the patties 3-4 minutes per side until golden brown.
  5. Drain on paper towels and serve warm or at room temperature.
