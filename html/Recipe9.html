Mediterranean Chickpea Salad

Ingredients

  • 200 g cooked chickpeas
  • 1 tomato
  • 1/2 cucumber
  • 1/4 red onion
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Lemon juice, parsley, salt, pepper

Instructions

  1. Drain the chickpeas. Wash and dice the tomato and cucumber.
  2. Finely slice the red onion.
  3. In a salad bowl, combine all ingredients.
  4. Dress with olive oil, lemon juice, salt, pepper, and chopped parsley.
  5. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving to allow flavors to meld.
