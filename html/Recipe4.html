Leftover Vegetable Muffins

Ingredients

  • 150 g cooked leftover vegetables (carrots, zucchini, leeks...)
  • 2 eggs
  • 100 g whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 packet baking powder
  • 50 ml milk (dairy or plant-based)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. In a bowl, beat the eggs with the milk and olive oil.
  3. Add the flour and baking powder, then mix until smooth.
  4. Fold in the chopped cooked vegetables. Season if necessary.
  5. Pour the batter into greased or lined muffin tins.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes until golden and risen. Let cool before removing from the tins.
