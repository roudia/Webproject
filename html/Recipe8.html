Baked Spiced Apples

Ingredients

  • 2 apples
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Optional: raisins or chopped nuts

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Wash the apples and core them carefully, keeping the base intact.
  3. Place the apples in a baking dish and stuff with raisins or nuts if desired.
  4. Sprinkle cinnamon over the apples.
  5. Add a little water to the bottom of the dish and bake for 25-30 minutes.
  6. Serve warm, optionally with yogurt.
